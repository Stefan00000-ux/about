About Us

Mindiva is a digital sanctuary created by Stephen Gill and Nityanand Tripathi, designed to comfort and support individuals dealing with trauma, anxiety, or depression. Our platform blends soothing visual experiences with mental wellness tools to help you feel at ease.

Our mission is to build a space where users can navigate their emotions, reflect, and feel supported. We incorporate modern animations, calming designs, and intuitive navigation to create a peaceful environment online.
